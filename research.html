Navigation

Our website has a straightforward navigation bar with a limited number of links, while the reference website has a drop-down menu for some links, providing a cleaner and more organized display. Product

The design of our website is simple and straightforward, with limited information and a solid colored background. The reference website includes a related product section, which is not available on our site. Additionally, the reference website is designed to be responsive, adjusting its layout for different screen sizes, while our website does not have this feature. In conclusion, the reference website has a more visually appealing and sophisticated product page, providing a better user experience, while our website has a more basic and functional approach.

Footer

Compared to the reference website, our website's footer is relatively simple and straightforward, with a limited number of links and a solid colored background. The reference website's footer is more visually appealing, with a larger number of links and a background image, and also has a newsletter subscription form like our website. In conclusion, the reference website's footer is more comprehensive and user-friendly, providing a better user experience, while our website's footer is more basic and functional..
